Honest status

Shipped today, and on the roadmap

CuraOS is pre-1.0 and in active development. Capability claims that are not yet generally available are framed as design intent, not shipped behavior.

Running today

  • Frontend apps on their own subdomains, one design system, dark mode and Arabic RTL that persist.
  • Neutral core plus Health and personal/business variants behind an API gateway.
  • OIDC single sign-on through Pocket-ID (Authorization Code with PKCE), brokered into a session.
  • Self-hosted runtime: Kubernetes, CNPG-managed PostgreSQL, Valkey, and an ingress controller.

On the roadmap

  • Read and write endpoints filling in across every service along the milestone plan.
  • The public demo tenant and the air-gap install bundle.
  • Education and ERP overlays beyond the Health first mover.
  • The 1.0 release: a stable, versioned contract surface.
